Epilepsy Warning:
=====================

Some people are liable to have an epileptic seizure or loss of consciousness when exposed to flashing lights or certain normal conditions of everyday life. Such persons could risk a seizure while watching television images, Playing video games or using our programs, and this can occur even if there have been no previous medical problems or experience of epilepsy. Please take the following general precautions when Playing video games and using our programs:


*Do not sit too close to the screen; position yourself with the linking cables at full stretch

*Play/Use the video games and programs preferably on a small screen.

*Do not play/use if you are tired or have not had much sleep.

*Make sure the room in which you are playing/using is well lit.

*Rest for 10-15 minutes per hour while playing/using video games and programs.
